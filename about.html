Our Mission

Cam-Bin is a non-profit app aimed at making trash sorting easier through a single, easy-to-use database. Simply type the name of the trash item and upload an image to figure out which bin it belongs in.

Beta Version

This app is currently in its beta version. We are continuously working on improvements and new features to make waste sorting smarter and more efficient.

Future Improvements

Future updates will include enhanced AI prediction, mobile support, and a broader trash database to make the app even more useful for everyday use.
